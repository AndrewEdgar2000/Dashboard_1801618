Plastic Pollution Dashboard
Plastic Waste Generated by Country in 2010
Packaging within the industrial sector is the sector which generates the most plastic waste. With over 140 million tonnes of plastic waste created each year.
The continent Asia has the highest share of plastic waste inputted into the worlds rivers.
The top three countries who produce the most plastic waste in the world are: China (59.1 million tonnes per year) USA (37.8 million tonnes per year) and Germany (14.5 million tonnes per year)
